⚠️  Free Tier Limitations:
   • Services sleep after 15 minutes of inactivity
   • Cold start takes 30-60 seconds on first request
   • 750 hours/month free (enough for 1 service 24/7)

🔒 Security:
   • Never commit .env files (already in .gitignore)
   • AWS credentials are stored securely in Render
   • Consider using IAM roles for production

💰 Costs:
   • Free tier: $0/month (with limitations)
   • Paid tier: $7/month per service (always-on)
   • AWS: Pay for DynamoDB/Cognito usage
